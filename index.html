Slash Commands

Six commands for every CRA workflow

Each command collects your inputs, applies CRA rules, and produces a structured, filing-ready output. Claude asks for any missing details in a single prompt.

/gst-filing

Calculates net GST/HST owing (or refund), breaks down every ITC by category, compares Quick Method vs. standard, and outputs the exact line numbers for CRA My Business Account filing.

Net tax calc ITC breakdown Quick Method comparison Lines 101/105/108/109
/gst-filing Q2 2026 — Ontario. Sales: $85,000 consulting + $15,000 products.
            Expenses: rent $5,000, software $2,000, meals $1,200, legal $3,000.
/payroll-calc

Calculates CPP, CPP2, EI, federal and provincial income tax for one pay period. Shows employee net pay, employer contributions, total CRA remittance, and flags when annual maximums will be reached.

Net pay Deduction breakdown CRA remittance Annual projection
/payroll-calc Ontario, bi-weekly, $3,500 gross, basic personal TD1 only
/payroll-calc BC, semi-monthly, $5,200 gross, TD1 federal $16,129 + spousal $14,156
/cra-deadlines

Generates every CRA deadline for your business profile: GST filings, payroll remittances, T4/T5s, corporate tax instalments, T2 filing, SR&ED. Flags the three highest-penalty-risk dates with specific penalty amounts.

12-month calendar Top 3 risk dates Penalty amounts Setup checklist
/cra-deadlines Ontario corporation, December 31 year-end, quarterly GST, 3 employees
/sred-screen

Applies the CRA three-part eligibility test (uncertainty, advancement, systematic investigation) to your project. Rates GREEN / YELLOW / RED, identifies documentation gaps, and estimates the refundable ITC cash value.

GREEN / YELLOW / RED rating CRA questions Doc gaps ITC estimate
/sred-screen We built a machine learning model to predict equipment failures.
             Existing models didn't generalize to our sensor data so we developed
             a custom architecture over 8 months of testing.
/grant-finder

Matches your business profile to federal and provincial grant programs ranked by expected value. Covers IRAP, Canada Job Grant, WES, CanExport, and province-specific programs across all regions.

Ranked programs Amounts & eligibility Application effort Quick wins
/grant-finder Ontario SaaS startup, 8 employees, $1.2M revenue,
              looking to fund developer hiring and AI R&D work
/t2-checklist

Generates a tailored T2 preparation checklist for your corporation's income type and special items. Covers required schedules, documents to gather, common CPA errors, and an accountant meeting timeline.

Document checklist Required schedules Common errors Filing timeline
/t2-checklist Ontario corp, March 31 year-end, active business income,
              purchased $40K equipment, paid $50K dividend to sole shareholder

Installation

Up and running in two steps

No API keys. No environment variables. No external services. Fully self-contained.

1

Install — one-time terminal step

Clone the repository and load it as a plugin when launching Claude Code.

# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/Srivatsa-Kasagar/canadian-tax-cra.git

# Launch Claude Code with the plugin loaded
claude --plugin ./canadian-tax-cra/plugin

Requires Claude Code CLI. Fully self-contained — no keys or env vars needed.

2

Use it — two ways, your choice

Once loaded, use structured slash commands or just ask in plain English. Both work.
